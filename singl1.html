CheeseBurger



  1. 2 pounds freshly ground chuck
  2. 1 tablespoon onion powder.
  3. 1 teaspoon salt.
  4. 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper.
  5. 12 slices deli-counter American cheese.
  6. 6 large burger buns, preferably homemade, toasted if desired.
